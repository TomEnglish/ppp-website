FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

General

What is contrast therapy?

Contrast therapy alternates between hot (sauna) and cold (plunge) exposure. This hot-cold cycling boosts circulation, reduces inflammation, speeds recovery, and delivers a powerful mood and energy boost.

What temperature is the cold plunge?

The Morozko maintains water between 32°F and 68°F. We typically set it to 38–42°F for optimal contrast therapy.

What temperature does the sauna reach?

The Clearlight infrared sauna reaches up to 170°F with full spectrum infrared heat.

Is cold plunge therapy safe?

Cold plunge therapy is generally safe for healthy adults. However, we recommend consulting your physician before use, especially if you have heart conditions, are pregnant, or have other health concerns. All users must sign a liability waiver.

What's the minimum age?

Users must be 16 or older. Minors require parent/guardian consent.

Drop-In Sessions

Where are your locations?

We operate at partner locations including gyms, CrossFit boxes, and wellness centers across the Upstate SC region. Check back for current sites and hours.

How do I book a drop-in session?

Select your location, choose an available time slot, and pay online. Just show up at your scheduled time — no equipment to bring, we have everything ready.

What's included in a drop-in session?

Access to the 4-person infrared sauna and 2-person cold plunge. Sessions are self-service.

Can I bring a friend?

Yes! The sauna fits 4 and the plunge fits 2. Book additional spots for your group.

How long is a session?

Standard drop-in sessions are 15–30 minutes. We also offer hourly rates ($40/hr) and extended time blocks with discounts.

Do you offer memberships?

Yes! Our Unlimited Monthly membership ($100) gives you unlimited sessions — the best value for regular plungers.

Event Rentals

What area do you serve for event rentals?

We deliver within a 50-mile radius of Campobello, SC, including Tryon, Landrum, Spartanburg, Greenville, and surrounding areas.

Is there a delivery fee?

Delivery is free within 20 miles of Campobello. Beyond that, fees are tiered by distance.

How much space do I need?

You’ll need a level surface that can accommodate our 8.5’ × 20’ trailer, plus room for safe access and setup.

Do I need a water hookup?

A standard garden hose connection is needed for the initial fill. We handle the rest.

What about electrical?

We require access to a 30A RV outlet or can discuss generator options. We’ll confirm requirements during booking.

How many people can use the equipment?

The sauna comfortably fits 4 people; the cold plunge accommodates 2 at a time. For larger events, guests rotate through.

How far in advance should I book?

We require a minimum of 3 days notice. For weekends, weddings, and events, we recommend booking 2–4 weeks ahead.

What's your deposit policy?

We require a 50% deposit to secure your date, with the balance due before your event.

What's your cancellation policy?

Cancel with 7 days notice for a full refund. We also offer full refunds for weather-related cancellations — we always err on the side of the customer.

Do you stay on-site during the rental?

Standard rentals are self-service after we set up and orient you. An on-site attendant is available as a paid add-on, and is included for corporate events and weddings.

What about bad weather?

You can cancel for weather with a full refund. We’ll work with you to reschedule if possible.

What's included in an event rental?

Delivery, setup, the full 4-person sauna + 2-person cold plunge experience, equipment orientation, and breakdown/pickup. See our packages for details.
