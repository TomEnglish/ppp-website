FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

Service Area & Delivery

What area do you serve?

We serve a 50-mile radius from Campobello, SC, including Tryon, Landrum, Spartanburg, Greenville, and surrounding areas.

Is there a delivery fee?

Delivery is free within 20 miles of Campobello. Beyond that, fees are tiered by distance.

Space & Setup Requirements

How much space do I need?

You'll need a level surface that can accommodate our 8.5' × 20' trailer, plus room for us to safely set up.

Do I need a water hookup?

A standard garden hose connection is needed for the initial fill. We handle the rest.

What about electrical?

We require access to a 30A RV outlet or generator. We can discuss alternatives during booking.

Capacity & Usage

How many people can use the sauna?

The sauna comfortably fits up to 4 people.

How many people can use the cold plunge?

The cold plunge accommodates up to 2 people at a time.

What temperature is the cold plunge?

The Morozko maintains water temperature between 32°F and 68°F. We typically set it to 38–42°F for optimal contrast therapy.

What temperature does the sauna reach?

The Clearlight infrared sauna reaches up to 170°F with full spectrum infrared heat.

Booking & Payment

How far in advance should I book?

We require a minimum of 3 days notice. For weekends and events, we recommend booking 1–2 weeks ahead.

What's your deposit policy?

We require a 50% deposit to secure your booking, with the balance due before your session.

What's your cancellation policy?

Cancel with 7 days notice for a full refund. We also offer full refunds for weather-related cancellations. We always err on the side of the customer.

Safety & Health

Is cold plunge therapy safe?

Cold plunge therapy is generally safe for healthy adults. However, we recommend consulting your physician before use, especially if you have heart conditions, are pregnant, or have other health concerns. All users must sign a liability waiver.

What's the minimum age?

Users must be 16 or older. Minors require parent/guardian consent.

Operations

Do you stay on-site during the rental?

Standard rentals are self-service with smart lock access. An on-site attendant is available as a paid add-on, and is included for corporate events and weddings.

What about bad weather?

You can cancel for weather with a full refund. We always err on the side of the customer.
